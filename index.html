Spatial Statistics Lab

Spatial Statistics is a sub-field of Statistics that focusses on development of statistical methodology suitable for analyzing spatial or spatio-temporal data. It requires modeling of dependency by spatial and/or temporal information.
Spatial data are observations that come with location information. A typical example is PM2.5 measurements at monitoring stations. When the data are observed temporally as well, they are called spatio-temporal data.
