Spatial Statistics Lab
(공간통계 연구실)

Spatial Statistics lab focusses on various methodological and application-driven projects that deal with spatial and spatio-temporal data. Spatial Statistics is a sub-area of Statistics which study spatial and spatio-temporal data by considering spatial/spatio-temporal uncertainty. It includes applications in various fields such as Public Health, Climatology, Environmetrics, Neuroscience, Biomedical engineering, Computer experiments, etc.
